Security

Your data is yours.
Not ours. Not anyone's.

We built BitBin on a simple principle: if you can't access someone's data, you can't be forced to hand it over. Your files are encrypted with AES-256 before they ever touch our servers.

  • AES-256 Encryption

    Military-grade encryption protects every file at rest and in transit.

  • Client-Side Encryption

    Files are encrypted in your browser before upload. We never see plaintext.

  • Secure Key Management

    Your encryption keys are derived from your password using industry-standard key derivation.

  • No Tracking, No Ads

    We don't track your behavior, sell your data, or show ads. Period.
